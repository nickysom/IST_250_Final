About Me

Thanks for stopping by my little photography studio! My name is Nick Som and we’re based in York, PA. I started photography when I was 12-13 years old, but I didn’t get my first professional camera till 17. I’ve done several professional photoshoots for friends, family, and many others! Come check out my portfolio, learn how to use a camera, or even book me for your events!

Why Photography?

I first started photography as a fun hobby and it still is for me. At first, I was just using my phone camera to capture different landscapes and objects. After saving enough money, I decided to invest in a professional camera and take better photos. Learning different camera settings and finding the right equipment was not easy. Luckily, I had a family member who was a professional photographer and helped me out. I also found YouTube videos and hands on experience really helpful. For now, I take fun, cute photos for friends and family. I do not claim to be a professional photographer, but I always enjoy working and learning new things.
